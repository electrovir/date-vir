Function diffDates

  • diffDates<const ChosenDurationUnits>(params): Duration<ChosenDurationUnits>

  • Diffs two dates and returns the diff in the specific unit.

    Note: when years, quarters, or months are used for the unit then "long term" durations are used to calculate the diff. See more details here: https://moment.github.io/luxon/#/math?id=casual-vs-longterm-conversion-accuracy
